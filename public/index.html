Oceanside's Most Authentic Thai Restaurant

Welcome to Thai Garden! We’re a cozy spot serving up delicious, authentic Thai food with flavors you won’t find anywhere else. Thai Garden is run by Chef Suk, an amazing cook and the friendliest person you’ll meet. She’s supported by Joy, who takes care of orders, serves guests, and keeps things running smoothly. Together, they’ve kept Thai Garden going strong, sharing their passion for great food and warm-heartedness. Come in for a meal and enjoy the unique charm that makes Thai Garden so welcoming!
